DMAIC Framework Implementation

🔴 DEFINE - Problem & Goal Statement

  • Problem: High employee attrition during Oct-Dec 2025 impacting cost, productivity, and workforce stability
  • Average Attrition: 4.6% per month (~55% annualized)
  • Goal: Reduce attrition by 20% in next 6 months, focusing on early-tenure employees

🟠 MEASURE - Data Collection & Baseline

  • Oct'25: HC 724 | Attrition 30 (4.14%)
  • Nov'25: HC 727 | Attrition 36 (4.95%)
  • Dec'25: HC 722 | Attrition 34 (4.71%)
  • Early Tenure Impact: 67% exits occur within first 6 months
  • DPM Calculation: (100 ÷ 2,173 × 1) × 1,000,000 = 45,999

🟡 ANALYZE - Root Cause Analysis

  • Pareto Analysis: Personal Reasons (55%), Other (24%), Career Growth (12%)
  • Key Finding: Early tenure attrition is the biggest contributor
  • Root Causes: Expectation mismatch, Workload/shift issues, Manager support gaps, Work-life balance issues, Stress

🟢 IMPROVE - Solutions Implemented

  • Early Engagement: Check-ins at 30, 60, 90 days
  • Manager Training: Capability enhancement programs
  • Role Clarity: Clear communication during onboarding
  • Target: 20% reduction in attrition (from 4.6% to 3.7% monthly)

🔵 CONTROL - Sustainability Plan

  • Monthly Dashboard: Attrition tracking with trend analysis
  • Tenure-based Monitoring: Early warning system for <6 months employees
  • Manager KPIs: Attrition linked to performance evaluations
  • Quarterly Reviews: HR leadership review of attrition metrics
  • Cost Savings: ₹67,640 annually (20% reduction target)

💡 Strategic Recommendations

Analyse Early Attrition

Investigate first 90-day turnover patterns. High early attrition suggests onboarding gaps, role clarity issues, or cultural misalignment requiring immediate intervention.

Shift to Forecast-Driven Hiring

Move from reactive (9-117 range) to strategic workforce planning. Implement predictive analytics to align hiring with business cycles and reduce volatility.

Strengthen Retention

Focus on manager effectiveness, career pathways, and engagement initiatives. Target the 7.8% attrition rate for reduction to industry benchmarks.

Track Net Movement

Implement monthly net headcount dashboards. Current trend shows -168 decline; establish early warning systems for workforce shrinkage risks.
